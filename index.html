Coding Journey

I am 25 years old. My journey into web development began with free resources on YouTube and other open platforms. As my interest grew, I enrolled in the Frontend Praktikum course on mohirdev.uz to deepen my skills. During the course, I completed over ten hands-on projects, which helped me develop the ability to build responsive and interactive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript based on Figma designs.

Later, I expanded my knowledge by learning popular CSS frameworks such as Bootstrap and Tailwind CSS, and built several projects using them. I also became familiar with essential tools and platforms like Git, GitHub, Vercel, and many other modern web development technologies.

Currently, I am enrolled in an advanced Next.JS course on sammi.ac, where I am continuing to strengthen my frontend development skills. With the knowledge and experience I have gained so far, I am capable of creating user-friendly, functional, and modern websites.
